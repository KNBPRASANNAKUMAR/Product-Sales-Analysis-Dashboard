📌 **Project Overview**

The **Product Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze product performance, customer purchasing behavior, regional sales contribution, and production cost efficiency.

This dashboard provides businesses with a comprehensive view of sales trends, customer activity, and profitability metrics through dynamic visualizations and KPI-driven analytics.

The project focuses on transforming raw transactional sales data into actionable business insights that support operational planning, pricing optimization, and strategic decision-making.



🎯 **Problem Statement**

Businesses often struggle to identify high-performing products, customer purchase trends, and profitable sales segments due to scattered transactional data and inefficient reporting systems.

Without proper business intelligence reporting, organizations face challenges in optimizing inventory, pricing strategies, and regional sales planning.

This dashboard was developed to solve the following business challenges:

- Analyze top-performing products based on unit sales
- Track customer purchasing behavior and buying trends
- Compare product pricing against production costs
- Identify state-wise purchasing contributions
- Monitor product profitability and operational efficiency
- Improve inventory and pricing decision-making


🧠 **Business Objectives**

- Analyze units sold by product category
- Evaluate customer purchasing behavior
- Compare production costs against selling prices
- Track regional sales contribution by state
- Monitor daily sales trends and demand patterns
- Build an interactive business intelligence sales dashboard


🔍 **Key Insights**

- **Multitool Survival Products** generated the highest unit sales among all product categories.
- Certain customers contributed significantly higher purchasing volumes compared to others.
- Production costs remained relatively lower for top-selling products, improving profitability margins.
- State-wise sales analysis revealed concentrated purchasing activity within specific regions.
- Daily purchasing trends demonstrated periodic spikes in product demand.
- Customer segmentation analysis highlighted high-value buyers contributing to revenue growth.


🧹 **Dataset Cleaning & Analysis**

🛠️ **Data Cleaning Process**

The dataset underwent preprocessing and transformation to improve analytical quality and reporting accuracy.

Key cleaning operations included:

- Removing duplicate transaction records
- Standardizing customer and product naming conventions
- Converting pricing fields into structured numerical values
- Cleaning incomplete sales records
- Formatting date fields for time-series analysis
- Structuring transactional metrics for KPI reporting

📈 **Data Analysis Performed**

The following analytical processes were implemented:

- Product-wise sales aggregation
- Customer segmentation analysis
- State-level revenue contribution analysis
- Production cost vs revenue comparison
- Trend analysis based on purchase dates
- Product profitability analysis
